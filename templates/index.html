BMW HISTORY TIMELINE

1916

BMW was founded in Munich, Germany as Bayerische Motoren Werke, producing aircraft engines.

1923

BMW’s first motorcycle, the R32, was introduced — a foundation for BMW’s engineering legacy.

1936

The legendary BMW 328 Roadster was born, setting new benchmarks in racing performance.

1956

The stunning BMW 507 Roadster captured hearts with its timeless elegance and design.

1972

The BMW 5 Series was introduced, marking the start of modern BMW luxury sedans.

1986

The first BMW M3 (E30) hit the roads, redefining performance and motorsport DNA.

2014

BMW launched the futuristic hybrid i8 — blending sustainability with performance.

2021

The all-electric BMW iX showcased BMW’s new generation of electric innovation.

2025

The Vision Neue Klasse concept defines BMW’s future — digital, electric, and dynamic.
